# Handstand Push-Up

Vertical pressing in a handstand. Use this as the step after elevated pike handstand push-ups are clean.

## How to perform (wall)

- Set up facing the wall: hands shoulder-width, fingers spread; walk or kick up until heels lightly touch the wall.
- Stack shoulders over wrists; ribs down; glutes and quads engaged.
- Lower until head lightly touches a mat or abmat; press to full lockout without losing line.

## Progression

- **Push A next step:** 3x3 wall handstand push-up paired with iron cross hold after elevated pike reps are clean.
- **Push B:** currently uses handstand control work: chest-to-wall hold -> wall walks -> freestanding hold.
- Regress: pike HSPU, box/wall partial ROM, or reduced depth with target height.

## Safety

- Do not train to form breakdown; stop if elbows flare uncontrollably or lower back arches excessively.
