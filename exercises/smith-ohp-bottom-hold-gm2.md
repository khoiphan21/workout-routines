# Overhead Press (Smith Machine) — Bottom Hold

Light **bottom-range hold** in the Push B overhead technical giant set (before heavy 3×3 Smith press).

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Seated or standing OHP on Smith; bar in bottom ~chin–ear range |
| **Load** | Light — **20–30 s** hold with **1–2 s in reserve** |

## How to perform

- Brace ribs down; do not over-arch. Hold without drifting forward.
- **3×20–30 s** duration sets, then proceed to D2 heavy triples in the same giant set.
