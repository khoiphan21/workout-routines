# Reverse Grip Lat Pulldown (Cable)

Vertical pull on the Gym Monster 2 (or any cable stack) with a supinated or parallel grip—substitutes for a plate-loaded high row when you only have cables and a calisthenics station.

## How to perform

- Sit tall or use a slight lean; start with arms extended, pull to upper chest or sternum without excessive torso swing.
- Control the eccentric; squeeze lats at the bottom of the movement.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x15, RPE 8–9, superset with cable crunch on Pull B.
