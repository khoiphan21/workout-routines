# Barbell Bench Press

Flat bench press for horizontal pressing strength. Used on Push B as the **power** lift (before handstand skills).

## How to perform

- Retract and depress the shoulder blades on the bench; maintain a slight arch that keeps ribs down.
- Grip slightly outside shoulder width (adjust for comfort and shoulder health).
- Touch the chest with control; press back over the shoulders in a slight arc.
- For **pause bench** (Push B): hold **2 s** on the chest on each work rep, staying tight—no bounce.

## Progression

- Power week: 3x3 at ~85–90% of a crisp triple, ~1 RIR on the first two work sets.
- Add 2.5–5 kg when all reps are clean with the prescribed pause.

## Scaling / safety

- Use spotter or safety arms when training near failure.
- If shoulder pain: narrow grip slightly, reduce range to pain-free depth, or substitute dumbbell bench until symptoms resolve.
