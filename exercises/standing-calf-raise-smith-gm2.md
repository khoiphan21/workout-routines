# Standing Calf Raise (Smith)

Gastrocnemius work on the GM2 Smith bar—Push B hypertrophy pairing with lateral raise.

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Bar on back (hooks + pad); balls of feet on floor or 1–3″ block |
| **Knees** | Almost straight |

## How to perform

- Full stretch at bottom, brief pause, strong top squeeze.
- **3×12**, RPE 8–9.
