# Overhead Press (Cable, Gym Monster 2)

Standing or high-kneeling overhead press on the Gym Monster 2 cable stack—same pattern as a barbell OHP without a dedicated shoulder press machine.

## How to perform

- Set cables just below shoulder height (or use a long strap handle); stand with a slight forward lean to match natural bar path, or kneel to reduce cheating.
- Brace ribs down, press to lockout without shrugging early; control the stack on the way down.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x8, RPE 8–9, superset with incline bench press (cable, GM2) on Push B.
