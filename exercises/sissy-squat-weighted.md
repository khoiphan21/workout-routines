# Sissy Squat (Weighted)

Knee-extension emphasis for quads using only body weight, a plate/belt, or a sissy squat bench—no leg-extension machine.

## How to perform

- Anchor feet and shins (sissy stand, Smith safeties low, or wedge against a fixed upright); torso stays tall, hips extend as knees bend.
- Descend under control; reverse before the low back rounds. Add load only when form stays crisp.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x12, RPE 8–9, superset with lateral raise (cable) on Push A.
