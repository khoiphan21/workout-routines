# Rings Hamstring Curl

⚙️ Technique:

- Lie on your back with your heels in the rings.
- Lift your hips off the ground, creating a straight line from your shoulders to your heels.
- Pull the rings towards you, bending your knees.
- Slowly return to the starting position.

🎯 Work prescription:

- 3x8
- Superset with Front Lever Hold.

📈 5 progression levels:

- Level 1: 3x8 with both feet in the rings.
- Level 2: 3x8 with one foot in the rings, the other leg bent.
- Level 3: 3x8 with one foot in the rings, the other leg straight.
- Level 4: 3x8 with one foot in the rings, the other leg straight and elevated.
- Level 5: 3x8 with one foot in the rings, with a pause at the top.

✅ Progress when:

- You can complete all prescribed reps without your hips dropping.

🪜 Scaling tools:

- Adjust the height of the rings to make the exercise easier or harder.
- Use a slider or a towel on a smooth surface as an alternative to rings.
- Perform the exercise with both feet on the ground to make it easier.
