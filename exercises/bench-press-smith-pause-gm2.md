# Bench Press (Smith Machine, 2s Pause)

Push B **power triple** with a **2 s chest pause**—replaces barbell bench from the rack in the home-gym program.

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Barbell hooks + pad; flat bench |
| **Grip** | Moderate width, wrists stacked over elbows |
| **Pause** | Bar motionless on chest **2 s** each rep |

## How to perform

- Touch chest, pause, then press without bouncing.
- **3×3** — ramp empty ×8–10, ~50%×3, ~70%×2, ~85%×1, work; **3–4 min** rest.
