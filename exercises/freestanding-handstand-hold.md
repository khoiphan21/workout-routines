# Freestanding Handstand Hold

Balance the handstand **without** wall contact. Use after chest-to-wall work when the straight line is consistent.

## How to perform

- From a safe kick-up or tuck-up, find balance on straight arms; fingers micromanage sway.
- Shoulders stacked over wrists; ribs down; legs together and active.

## Prescription (Push B)

- **Power week:** 3×15 s (accumulate clean time—if you bail, restart the set). Rest 2–3 min.
- Prefer many short clean holds over long sloppy ones.

## Scaling

- Use fingertip spot against wall, or hybrid “one-foot light touch” until balance improves.
- Parallettes or blocks optional if wrist comfort is an issue.
