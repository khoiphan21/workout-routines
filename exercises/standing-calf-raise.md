# Standing Calf Raise

Gastrocnemius work on the floor, a low block, or the edge of your bench—no standing calf machine required.

## How to perform

- Ball of foot on a 1–3 inch rise if available; full stretch at bottom, brief pause, strong top squeeze.
- Knees almost straight; add a light bar on the back only if you already use it at your station.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x12, RPE 8–9, superset with cable lateral raise on Push B.
