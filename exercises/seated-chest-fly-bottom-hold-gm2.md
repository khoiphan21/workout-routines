# Seated Chest Fly (Cable) — Bottom Hold

Light **stretch hold** in the Push A technical block (paired with Smith OHP 3×3).

## Pulley setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Pulleys** | **~level 4–6** (mid), vertical bars |
| **Attachment** | Handles |
| **Position** | Seated on bench, slight forward lean |

## How to perform

- Hold the bottom stretch **10 s** with **1–2 s in reserve**; squeeze chest without shoulder pain.
- **3×10 s** duration sets, light load only.
