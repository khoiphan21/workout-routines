# Standing Calf Raise (Machine)

Calf-gastrocnemius work on a standing calf machine or Smith (GM2-compatible stack if available).

## How to perform

- Full stretch at the bottom, pause briefly, drive through the ball of the foot to a strong top contraction.
- Keep knees soft but not bent excessively.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x12, RPE 8–9, superset with cable lateral raise on Push B.
