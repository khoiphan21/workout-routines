# Sissy Squat (Cable, Gym Monster 2)

Knee-extension quad work on the GM2 cable stack—replaces **Single Leg Press** and mirrors home-gym **weighted sissy squat** (anchored feet, cable load).

## Pulley setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Pulleys** | Low on vertical bars, **~level 1–3** (same side) |
| **Attachment** | Smart PowerGrip handle (one hand) or belt/strap clipped to low cable |
| **Anchor** | Balls of feet against Smith upright or fixed wedge; torso tall |

## How to perform

- Hold the handle at chest or wear a belt to the low cable for counterbalance/load.
- Descend with hips extended and knees forward; reverse before low back rounds.
- **3×12/side** (or alternate legs each set), RPE 8–9 on Push A hypertrophy block.
