# Incline Bench Press (Cable, Gym Monster 2)

Low incline (~30–45°) pressing on a bench with GM2 cables—upper chest emphasis without an incline machine stack.

## How to perform

- Bench facing the stack; cables low so the line matches an incline bar path at the bottom of the rep.
- Retract and depress slightly; press with elbows ~45° from torso; full ROM without flaring.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x12, RPE 8–9, paired after cable OHP on Push B.
