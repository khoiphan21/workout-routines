# Bulgarian Split Squat (Cable, Gym Monster 2)

Single-leg squat with rear foot elevated—loaded via low cable on the GM2 stack.

## Pulley setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Pulleys** | **~level 2–4** on vertical bars (low) |
| **Attachment** | One Smart PowerGrip handle (same-side hand as front leg) |
| **Bench** | Rear foot on Works / Works Plus |

## How to perform

- Front foot far enough that knee tracks over mid-foot at the bottom.
- Stay tall; load through the front leg. **3×8/side**, RPE 8–9 (Push A H1).
