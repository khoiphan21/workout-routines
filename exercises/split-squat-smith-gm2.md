# Split Squat (Smith Machine)

Conditioning-day quad work on the Smith bar—light load, higher reps.

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Bar on back (hooks + pad) or goblet-style front rack if preferred |
| **Stance** | Split stance; torso tall |

## How to perform

- Drop back knee toward floor; drive through front leg.
- **Circuit:** 12 reps/side × 3 rounds at RPE ~5–7.
