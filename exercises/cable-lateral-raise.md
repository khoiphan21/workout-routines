# Lateral Raise (Cable)

Side-delt isolation on a cable stack (Gym Monster 2 or crossover). Used on **Push A** so **Push B** can use the prone incline Y-raise + incline press giant and triceps isolation without duplicating lateral work.

## Pulley setup (GM2)

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Pulleys** | **Floor platform** anchors (both sides, low platform—not vertical bars) |
| **Attachment** | Smart PowerGrip handle (one or two; cross-body or bilateral as prescribed) |

On a crossover, use the lowest pulley setting instead.

## How to perform

- Slight hinge; ribs down; raise to shoulder height with soft elbows.
- Control the lowering phase; avoid shrugging.

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 3x12, RPE 8–9, superset with Bulgarian split squat on Push A.
