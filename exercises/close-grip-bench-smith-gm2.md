# Close-Grip Bench Press (Smith Machine)

Push A **power triple** on the GM2 Smith—replaces weighted dip from the home-gym program.

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Barbell hooks + pad; bench under Smith path |
| **Grip** | ~shoulder width (close, not ultra-narrow) |
| **Feet** | Planted; slight arch OK |

## How to perform

- Lower to lower chest/upper abs; press to lockout.
- **3×3** — ramp empty ×8–10, ~50%×3, ~70%×2, work; **3–4 min** rest; **+2.5 kg** after clean 3×3.
