# Overhead Press (Cable, Gym Monster 2) — Top Hold

Light **lockout hold** after Smith OHP triples in the Push B technical giant set.

## Pulley setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Pulleys** | **~level 6–8** (high-mid) on vertical bars |
| **Attachment** | Handles or short strap |

## How to perform

- Press to top range; hold **15 s** with light load and clean ribs-down brace.
- **3×15 s** duration sets; **60–75 s** after the full D1→D2→D3 round.
