# Romanian Deadlift (Smith Machine)

Hip-hinge **power triple** on Pull B—replaces heavy close-grip lat pulldown (which caps on kneeling GM2 pulldowns without a knee brace).

## Smith setup

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Mode** | Barbell hooks + pad on Smith arms |
| **Stance** | Hip-width, soft knees, bar over mid-foot |
| **Grip** | Pronated, just outside legs |

## How to perform

- Hinge until hamstrings stretch (bar stays close to legs); neutral spine.
- Drive hips forward to stand; avoid turning it into a squat.
- **Power:** 3×3 — ramp empty bar ×8–10, ~50%×3, ~70%×2, work sets; **3–4 min** rest; **+2.5 kg** after clean 3×3.
