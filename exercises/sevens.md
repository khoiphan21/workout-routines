# Sevens

Bodyweight ab circuit used anywhere the programs previously used Cable Crunch. Log one completed circuit as one rep in Hevy.

## Circuit

- 7 leg raises
- 7 bicycle crunches
- 7 leg raises
- 7 bicycle crunches
- 7 seconds boat hold
- 7 leg raises
- 7 side-to-side toe touches
- 7 leg raises
- 7 side-to-side toe touches
- 7 seconds boat hold

## Prescription

- **Power Week:** 1 circuit, controlled reps, clean bracing throughout.
