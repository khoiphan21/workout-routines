# Weighted Chin-Up

⚙️ Technique:

- Grip the bar with your hands shoulder-width apart, palms facing towards you.
- Pull yourself up until your chin is over the bar.
- Lower yourself back down to the starting position with control.
- Keep your core engaged and avoid swinging.

🎯 Work prescription:

- **Power Week main split:** Pull B uses **muscle-up** as the heavy slot; add **weighted chin-ups** as optional extra volume if you want more supinated heavy work.
- Otherwise: **3×3** as a primary power pull when programmed.

📈 5 progression levels:

- Level 1: 3x5 bodyweight chin-ups.
- Level 2: 3x3 bodyweight chin-ups.
- Level 3: 3x5 with added weight.
- Level 4: 3x3 with added weight.
- Level 5: 3x3 with heavier weight.

✅ Progress when:

- You can complete all prescribed reps with a full range of motion.

🪜 Scaling tools:

- Use a resistance band for assistance.
- Perform negative chin-ups to build strength.
- Use a low bar or rings for partial ROM or foot-assisted reps if you do not have a commercial assisted machine.
