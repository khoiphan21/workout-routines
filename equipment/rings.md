# Rings

Gymnastic rings with adjustable straps for suspension training. Used for rows, push-ups, support holds, and skill progressions.

## Typical features

- Pair of rings suspended from straps
- Adjustable height via straps
- Can be hung from pull-up bar, ceiling mount, or stand

## Used for

- Ring rows, ring push-ups, ring flyes
- Support holds, L-sit, body saw
- Front lever, back lever, iron cross progressions
- Hamstring curls, knee tucks
