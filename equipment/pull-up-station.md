# Pull-Up Station

A freestanding or wall-mounted station with a horizontal bar for pull-ups, chin-ups, and other hanging exercises.

## Typical features

- Horizontal bar at height suitable for full hang
- May include multiple grip positions (narrow, wide, neutral)
- Often part of a power tower or standalone bar

## Used for

- Pull-ups, chin-ups, weighted pull-ups
- Hanging knee raises, L-sit holds
- Front lever progressions, muscle-ups (with sufficient clearance)
