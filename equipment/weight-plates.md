# Weight Plates

Standard weight plates for loading barbell and bodyweight exercises. Total capacity up to 40 kg.

## Typical setup

- Multiple plates (e.g. 2.5 kg, 5 kg, 10 kg, 20 kg) to reach total load up to 40 kg
- Used with a weight belt for weighted pull-ups, dips, and chin-ups
- Can also be used for goblet squats, plate rows, or other loaded exercises

## Used for

- Weighted pull-ups, weighted chin-ups, weighted dips
- Progressive overload on pull and push movements
