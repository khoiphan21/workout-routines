# Dip Station (P-Bars)

Parallel bars (P-bars) for dips, L-sits, and support holds. May be freestanding or part of a power tower.

## Typical features

- Two parallel bars at shoulder height or slightly wider
- Adjustable or fixed width
- Sturdy for bodyweight and weighted dips

## Used for

- Dips (chest, triceps)
- Weighted dips (with weight belt)
- L-sit holds, support holds
- Ring support (if rings are hung from a separate structure)
