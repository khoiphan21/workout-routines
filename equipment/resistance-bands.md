# Resistance Bands

Elastic bands providing variable resistance for strength and assistance work. Available in multiple tension levels.

## Tensions

- **5 kg** — Light; warm-ups, band pull-aparts, shoulder mobility
- **10 kg** — Light–medium; assistance work, band rows
- **25 kg** — Medium; band-assisted pull-ups, band rows, lateral raises
- **37.5 kg** — Medium–heavy; heavy band rows, band pulldowns
- **50 kg** — Heavy; maximum assistance or resistance for strong movements

## Used for

- Band pull-aparts, dislocates, banded rows
- Band-assisted pull-ups, dips, push-ups
- Band lateral raises, triceps pressdowns, face pulls
- Band RDLs, band squats, banded hip thrusts
