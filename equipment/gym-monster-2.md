# Gym Monster 2 (Speediance)

An all-in-one smart home gym with electromagnetic resistance, combining a power cage, Smith machine, squat rack, bench rack, cable machine, and pulley system in a compact unit.

## Overview

- **Brand:** Speediance
- **Product:** [Gym Monster 2](https://speediance.com.au/products/speediance-gym-monster-2)
- **Resistance:** Up to 100 kg (220 lbs), adjustable in 0.5 kg increments
- **Type:** Electromagnetic resistance (no weight plates or cables)

## Resistance modes

- **Standard mode** — Consistent resistance throughout the movement
- **Eccentric mode** — Increased resistance on the negative (return) phase
- **Chain mode** — Gradually increasing resistance as you approach the top
- **Fixed speed mode** — Constant speed for stable, rehab-focused training

## Attachments and accessories

### Benches

- **Works 2.0** — Flat bench for classic strength workouts
- **Works Plus 2.0** — Adjustable incline bench
- **AeroRow / AeroRow 2** — Rowing bench for horizontal pulling and cardio
- **Family 2.0** — Flat bench + rowing bench
- **Family Plus 2.0** — Adjustable bench + rowing bench

### Cable and handles

- **Barbell hooks** — For barbell-style movements
- **Barbell pad** — For comfort on barbell exercises
- **Tricep rope** — For tricep pushdowns and extensions
- **Smart PowerGrip handles** — Standard cable handles
- **Skiing handles** — For rowing and pulling variations
- **Ankle straps** — For leg curls, kickbacks, hip work

### Other

- **Angle adapter** — Extends the pulley’s range of motion at the lower platform
- **Accessory rack** — Storage for attachments
- **Yoga mat** — Floor work
- **Weightlifting belt** — For heavy lifts

## Cable system

- **11 adjustable height settings** — Cable anchors from floor level to the main frame (levels 0–9)
- **Dual direct-drive motors** — Electromagnetic resistance
- **Smart safety system** — Sensors cut resistance when unsafe motion is detected

## Used for

- **Rowing** — Horizontal pulling with AeroRow bench
- **Cable rows** — Bent-over rows, single-arm rows, high-to-low rows
- **Pulldowns** — Lat pulldowns, straight-arm pulldowns
- **Curls** — Bicep curls, hammer curls
- **Tricep work** — Tricep pushdowns, extensions (with tricep rope)
- **Wood chops** — Single-arm wood chops, rotational work
- **Leg work** — Leg curls, kickbacks, hip work (with ankle straps)
- **Squats and presses** — With barbell hooks and bench
- **Smith machine movements** — Squats, bench press, overhead press

## Substitutes for

- Band rows, band pulldowns, band high-to-low rows
- Ring rows (with AeroRow or cable setup)
- Cable machine equivalents for most upper-body and some lower-body exercises
